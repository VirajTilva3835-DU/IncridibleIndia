Essential Travel Tips

📅

Plan Wisely

Check local weather, seasonal festivals, and operating hours before booking destinations.

🙏

Respect Culture

Dress modestly and follow local customs when visiting religious sites and heritage monuments.

🚗

Safe Mobility

Use pre-booked taxis, government-approved guides, or popular ridesharing apps in major cities.

💳

Payments & UPI

Carry some cash for local vendors, though digital UPI payments are widely accepted across most shops.
